Our Programme

 

 

 

 

 

The Pioneer Explorer Scouts was launched in June 2003, following the Scout Associations “PRI” initiative. Catering for boys & girls from Rochdale District’s Scout Troops, The Pioneer Unit will accept any older Scout between the ages of 14 & 18. We will also happily accept recruits from outside the movement.

 

NEWS:

 

Growing Numbers: The Unit continues to grow in size. New meetings are to be set up soon at Wardle, Norden & Balderstone. We also plan to be regular service team members at Ashworth Valley.

 

New Badges: HQ has recently announced changes to our Activity Badges, Please look on “Scoutbase” for details.

 

Our first meeting was held at the HQ of First Rochdale Scout Group, the time the third oldest Scout Group in the world. Meetings were held once a month at first, on the last Friday of the month in question. Our second meeting was held at Ashworth Valley, our local campsite. Where we used various activities on site.

 

We meet at the 29thA Rochdale, at Dearnley, near Littleborough. At the 10th Rochdale, Norden, the 17th Rochdale, Wardle, & at the 37th Rochdale, Balderstone/Sudden. For meeting times, please contact the Unit from the Programme page.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

We also have Young Leaders, who work within the sections at many of our Groups around the District.

 

This Website is by & for the Explorer Scouts in our Unit. If you have anything to put onto it, please let us know. You can e mail it to us, or put it on a floppy disc. You can even come round & put it up yourself!

 

The programme of activities we use are based around the following eight programme zones:

  • Outdoors
  • Skills
  • Physical Recreation
  • Community Service
  • Environment
  • International
  • Relationships
  • Values
